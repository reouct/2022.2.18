Los Angeles Lakers

[The Los Angeles Lakers are a professional basketball team located in Los Angeles, California, USA, belonging to the Western Pacific Division of the NBA. The team, formerly known as the "Minneapolis Lakers", won 5 championships during the Minneapolis period. After the team moved to Los Angeles in 1959, it has won a total of 17 championships, and has feuded with the Celtics. tied for first place in the league.]
